The C++ class library: http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/paintlib-2.5.0.zip (about 1,1 mb) or http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/paintlib-2.5.0.tar.gz (about 800 kb).

Download this file. In addition to it, download libpng, zlib, libtiff, libungif and libjpeg if you want png, tiff, gif and jpeg support. See the links page for locations of these libraries.

paintX: http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/paintx.zip (about 200 kb)

You can also download the suite of test images: http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/testpic.zip or http://www.paintlib.de/paintlib/testpic.tar.gz (about 1 mb either way).
